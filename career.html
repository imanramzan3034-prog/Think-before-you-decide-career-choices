Career:

A career is a long-term profession or work path that a person chooses based on their interests,skills, and education. Unlike a job, a career focuses on growth, experience, and future goals.

Why Career Choice Matters

Choosing a career is one of the most important decisions of life.

Advantages:

  • Future security
  • Personal satisfaction
  • Growth and Sucess
  • Contribution to society
  • Long-term Impact

Disadvantages:

  • Missed Opportunities
  • Slow Growth
  • Regret
  • Dissatisfaction
  • High Stress
  • Poor Earning
  • Low Confidence and Motivation

Career Options:

There are so many career options. Some popular and practical career options are:

  1. Technology & Digital Careers
  2. Medical & Health Sciences
  3. Engineering & Technology
  4. Arts, Design & Media
  5. Education & Academia
  6. Business & Management
  7. Sports & Entertainment
